Rules :
  1. The game is played by two players.
  2. Each player takes turns rolling a six-sided dice.
  3. If a player rolls a 6 , they get an additional turn to roll the dice.
  4. If a player rolls a 1, 5 points are deducted from their score and awarded to their opponent.
  5. The first player to reach 30 points wins the game.
